Renewable Energy is the key to human's future

Energy Development is the key to economic growth and the foundation of a country. As we have more people on earth, we will need more energy for all aspects of life. It is very important to study the sources of energy, how it is extracted, its usage and its effect on the world as a whole

For decades, we have been relying on fossil fuels for electricity generation and the challenge to fossil fuels is climate change, pollution and limited resources. In the new century, we need change to renewable energy for a brighter future. Renewable Energy will help build a stable and sustainable environment for humamity to thrive and prosper.

The potential to develop renewable energy in the US is huge. Hydroelectricity has been the major contributor in renewable energy until recent years. Price for installing Solar panel and Windmill is decreasing rapidly. Sooner or later, Wind energy and Solar energy will take over the role of Hydro electricity to be the main renewable energy sources and then they will replace fossil fuels to generate electricity, Lots of resources to harness along with dropping price will pave the way for Wind and Solar energy development.

A myth about renewable energy like wind and solar is that they are expensive. However, in recent years price for installing Solar PV and Windmill is dropping dramatically while the cost of installing natural gas generator remains the same.
